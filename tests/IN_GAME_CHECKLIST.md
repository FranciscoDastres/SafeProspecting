# In-Game Checklist

Target clients:

- WoW TBC Classic Anniversary 2.5.5 (`Interface 20505`)
- WoW Mists of Pandaria Classic 5.5.x (`Interface 505xx`)

1. Install the full `SafeProspecting` folder under the active client AddOns path (`_anniversary_` or `_mists_`).
2. Log in on a character with Jewelcrafting and Prospecting.
3. Put at least one stack of 5+ supported ore in the bags.
4. Mouse over the ore and confirm the tooltip shows possible prospecting results.
5. Confirm the bag slot has the SafeProspecting overlay.
6. Left-click the minimap button to show and hide the action panel.
7. Click the action panel once and confirm exactly one prospecting cast starts.
8. Loot the result if a loot window opens.
9. Confirm the queue advances only after the spell succeeds and the ore stack count changes.
10. Test one stack of 4 ore and confirm it is not marked.
11. Test Khorium Ore and confirm it is not marked.
12. On MoP Classic, test Ghost Iron Ore, Kyparite, Black Trillium Ore, and White Trillium Ore.
